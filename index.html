    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is a resume checker?
    A resume checker evaluates and improves resumes by checking formatting, ATS keywords, grammar errors, and content relevance.
    How do I check my resume score?
    Upload your resume and job description into Resume Analyzer PRO. Our AI system instantly calculates your resume score.
    How do I improve my resume score?
    Improve missing skills, match keywords with job description, and ensure proper formatting for ATS compliance.
    How do I know my resume is ATS compliant?
    Ensure your resume uses simple formatting, relevant keywords, and avoids images or complex layouts.
    What is a good ATS score?
    A score above 70% is generally considered competitive for most roles.
    Can an ATS read PDFs?
    Yes, most modern ATS systems can read text-based PDFs, but avoid scanned image PDFs.
